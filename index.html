📖 Block Blast Mini Game Basics

Block Blast Mini is a free browser-based puzzle game inspired by the popular Block Blast. The goal is simple — place block shapes on the grid and clear as many lines as possible.

How to Play

  • Each round you receive 3 block pieces of random shapes.
  • Drag a piece onto the 8×8 board to place it in a valid position.
  • You must place all 3 pieces before a new set appears.
  • When a full row or column is completed, it clears and you earn points.
  • Clear multiple lines at once for a combo bonus.
  • The game ends when no remaining piece can fit on the board.

Tips & Strategy

  • Keep the center of the board open for larger pieces.
  • Plan ahead — think about all three pieces before placing the first.
  • Avoid stacking blocks in corners early, as they become hard to clear.
  • Prioritise setting up multi-line clears for bigger combo scores.
